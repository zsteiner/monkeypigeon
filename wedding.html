The Wedding

Everything you ever wanted to know about Zach and Jackie’s wedding*

* but were afraid to ask

So... Where is Eagle?

Eagle, CO is roughly two hours West of Denver. Visit our Getting Here page for details on traveling to Eagle and our Staying in the Area page for information on lodging.

Should I rent a car?

Probably. Public transport is available in Eagle County and the surrounding areas, but there aren’t stops near the house.

What should I wear?

Whatever you want! We’re planning to dress up a little (we are getting married), but we don’t care if you do. We just want everyone to be comfortable and have a good time.

Weather permitting, the ceremony will be outside, so... maybe don’t wear a fur coat?

Does the wedding have a theme?

The wedding is elegant-nautical-vintage-steampunk-woodland-beach-rustic-modern-Simpsons-bohemian-themed.

When should I arrive?

We’re planning to start the ceremony around 3:00pm, so anywhere in the 2:30 – 2:59pm range would be great!

What’s the plan for the reception?

Hors d’oeuvres, delicious beer, wine, boozy punch, and cake until 6:00pm or thereabouts, after which there will be extended feasting and socializing. Stay or go – do what you feel, man!

Will there be dancing?

There won’t be a dance floor, but there will be liquor and music, so... maybe?

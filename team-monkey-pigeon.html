Team Monkey-Pigeon

A little over four years ago...

... fate, luck, and a super-sophisticated computer algorithm brought Zach (Pigeon) and Jackie (Monkey) together.

Here are some fun facts to help you get to know the happy couple better:

Location, location, location.
  • Zach and Jackie met while living in Fort Collins, Colorado.
  • They moved to Indianapolis, IN (where they now reside) together in late May of 2012. Though the move was difficult, they like to think it brought them closer.
It’s all about food... and drinks.
  • Jackie’s favorite thing that Zach makes is spaghetti and meatballs.
  • Zach’s favorite thing that Jackie makes is dessert (Jackie proposed to Zach with a strawberry rhubarb pie—with a crumb top!)
  • Zach and Jackie both love good beer—they had their first date at the Crown Pub in Fort Collins, CO.
Monkey and Pigeon look forward to spending their lives as married folks ...
  • Hiking! Zach and Jackie love to hike. Indiana’s pretty flat, but they manage to find lots of excuses to tromp around the forest.
  • Cooking dinner together and finding new favorite recipes.
  • Going on car-trips and adventuring together.
  • Finding new beers to try.

Why Monkey-Pigeon?

In the Fall of 2011, Jackie came upon a photograph of a baby macaque hugging a pigeon.

Later that week, Zach hugged Jackie and she exclaimed, “I’m as happy as a monkey holding a pigeon!”. Zach and Jackie jokingly started calling each other Monkey and Pigeon, and it stuck.

Team Monkey-Pigeon was born!

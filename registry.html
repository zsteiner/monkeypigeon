Registry

If you’d like to give a gift...

First, know that though we appreciate gifts, we don’t expect them. We just want you to come and celebrate with us! Your presence at our wedding is a pretty terrific gift itself.

For those that simply must give a gift, we registered with Thankful Registry. It’s a service that allows you to register for items across sellers, rather than being limited to 1-2 stores.

Since everything in the registry is available for purchase online, we would appreciate having items shipped to our home rather than brought to the ceremony. As much fun as unwrapping presents can be(!!!!), shipping them from Colorado to Indiana is less so.

Staying in the Area

There are a lot of lodging options in the greater Eagle area.

Here are the 5 best hotels in Eagle according to TripAdvisor. Neighboring towns of Gypsum and Edwards also have ample lodging. Both are about a 20 minute drive from Jackie's parents' home.

In addition to hotels, there are a lot of vacation rentals in the Eagle area. Summer is the off season, so houses and condos can be rented for comparable nightly rates to hotels. Rentals can be found via Airbnb and HomeAway.
